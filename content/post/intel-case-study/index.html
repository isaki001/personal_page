--- title: My work featured at www.intel.com date: '2023-11-27' summary: PAGANI & m-CUBES: An Effortless Migration from CUDA to SYCL for Numerical Integration --- [Click here for article](https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/developer/articles/case-study/pagani-and-m-cubes-migration-from-cuda-to-sycl.html) I used oneAPI to increase the portability capabilities of my integrators. I originally implemented PAGANI and m-Cubes in CUDA, which allows users to utilize NVIDIA GPUs to integrate multi-dimensional integrals. With oneAPI I was able to expand the list of compatible hardware and I demonstrated that you can achieve the same performance with CUDA with no more than 15% performance penalty.
